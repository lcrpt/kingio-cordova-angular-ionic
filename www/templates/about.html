How to Play



Touch & Move

Controls

You can only move the postition of the king one cell at the time.

Tabs Actions

Controls

- Lives : number of lives you have, you can get additional lives if they spawn on the map
- Bombs : number of bombs you have, you can get additional bombs if they spawn on the map. By using the bomb, you destroy a random enemy.
- Teleport : you can teleport your king in a random free cell at any moment.
- Restart : restart the game at any moment.
- Quit : quit the game at any moment.

Customs Settings

Settings

- Music : you can turn off or on some sweet music.
- Game Volume : you can turn off or on some game volume effect.
- Colorways : you can change colorways depending on your mood.
